What is Yoga?

Yoga is a spiritual and physical practice rooted in Indian philosophy that has existed for thousands of years.There are several different styles of yoga that range from gentle, meditative experiences to more physically intense formats. The mind-body exercise typically consists of doing sequences of physical poses combined with deep breathing techniques.

Defining 'yoga' is a bit like trying to define a grand concept like spirituality or culture. Any practice, both internal and external, that weaves intention, knowledge and affirmative agency can be yoga.

Why do Yoga?

  • Overall Wellbeing
  • Stress Relief
  • Fountain of Youth
  • Enhanced Strength and Flexibility
